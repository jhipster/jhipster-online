JHipster Online is also Open Source!

The application on https://start.jhipster.tech/ is the official JHipster Online instance, which is managed by the JHipster team.

If you want to look at our source code, or if in your company wants to host (and modify!) its own version of JHipster Online, the project is available on GitHub at https://github.com/jhipster/jhipster-online.
